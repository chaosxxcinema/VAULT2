**Not just inventory management. A memory vault for your life's slices.**

VAULT2 is designed to eliminate the friction of organizing personal items (like skincare, supplements, or anything with confusing foreign labels). Just snap a photo, and let our local OCR engine automatically extract the text (supports multiple languages). No more tedious typing—just a beautiful, nostalgic timeline of what you use and how long it has accompanied you.

### What kind of project is this?
VAULT2 is a pure front-end web app built with an absolute "Local-First" philosophy. 
No servers, no account systems, and currently, no external AI APIs. 

### 👁️ Local OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
Instead of relying on costly AI Vision APIs, VAULT2 uses a local OCR system. 
- **Zero Cost & Offline:** You only need to download the OCR language data on your very first use. After that, text recognition happens entirely within your device. 
- **Total Privacy:** Your photos and data never leave your browser. 
*(Note: Advanced AI integration is currently reserved for potential future commercial versions.)*

### 🚀 Getting Started

**Just click and use — no download needed:**
👉 [Open VAULT2](#) *(replace `#` with your actual GitHub Pages URL after enabling it)*

Snap a photo of your item's label and let the local OCR do the rest. The first time you use it, your browser downloads the OCR language pack (one-time) — after that, everything runs **fully offline**, right in your browser.

📱 **On your phone:** open the link above, then use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" — it'll sit on your phone like a regular app icon, and works even without a signal once the language pack is cached.

*(Prefer to keep a local copy instead? You can also download `index.html` from this repo and open it directly in your browser — same thing, just offline-hosted.)*

**VAULT2 解決的不是管理，是遺忘。一個抵抗遺忘的個人記憶倉庫。**

不再需要手打繁瑣的英文與外語標籤，也不用面對冷冰冰的表格。拿起手機拍一張照，讓本地 OCR 系統瞬間為你提取文字，留下這個物品陪伴你的時間線。每一次替換，都是你生命某個階段的切片。

### 這是什麼樣的專案？
VAULT2 是一個純前端網頁應用，堅持絕對的「本地優先（Local-first）」極簡哲學。
沒有伺服器、沒有帳號系統，目前也不依賴任何外部 AI API。

### 👁️ 本地 OCR 識別系統
為了降低使用門檻並保護隱私，VAULT2 目前不採用雲端 AI 視覺，而是內建了強大的本地 OCR 引擎。
- **免費且支援離線：** 只需要在第一次使用時下載一次 OCR 識別包（支援多語言）。之後的所有文字識別都在你的設備本地完成，無需網路，無需 API Key！
- **絕對隱私：** 你的照片和生活碎片只存在你自己的手機裡，哪裡都不會去。
*（註：進階的 AI 智能識別系統暫不開放，未來將保留至商業授權版本中使用。）*

### 🚀 怎麼開始用

**點連結就能用，不用下載：**
👉 [打開 VAULT2](#)（開通 GitHub Pages 後把 `#` 換成你實際的網址）

拍一張物品標籤的照片，剩下的交給本地 OCR。第一次使用時，瀏覽器會下載一次 OCR 語言包（只需一次）——之後**完全離線**都能用，全部在你的瀏覽器裡跑。

📱 **手機上使用：** 打開上面的連結，用瀏覽器的「加入主畫面」功能，就會像一般 App 一樣躺在你的手機桌面上，語言包快取好之後，就算沒訊號也能用。
